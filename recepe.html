HOW TO MAKE EGG CURRY



INGREDIANTS

  • eggs
  • onions
  • one cup of oil
  • red chillies
  • salt
  • ginger paste


PROCEDURE

  1. take oil in a pan and heat it gently
  2. add onions
  3. then mix onions with the ginger paste
  4. add chilly powder to that
  5. add required amount of salt
  6. add eggs to the final product and keep it on the stove for a while
  7. then your curry is ready to serve
